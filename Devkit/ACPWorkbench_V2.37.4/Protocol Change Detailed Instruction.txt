[V2.21.1]

1. 音效参数：
   ECHO, PingPong, PCMDelay三个音效的参数个数发生变化；

For afore content, the translation is as following:

[V2.21.1]

1. Sound effect parameters:
The number of parameters for the three sound effects, ECHO, PingPong, and PCMDelay, has changed.

2. Export file:
1) If the sound effects exported from versions prior to V2.21.0 contain ECHO, PingPong or PCMDelay, the use of this version is not supported.
2) If the sound effects exported from versions prior to V2.21.0 do not include ECHO, PingPong or PCMDelay, support is applicable to that version.
